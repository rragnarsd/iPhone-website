iPhone features

Discover the latest technology

Send and receive payments via Apple Pay. Power up your phone with wireless charging. Unlock the latest iPhone models just by looking at them.Worried about spending too much time on your phone? Don’t be. With the new Screen Time feature, you can see how much time you’re spending in each app, and set limits if you want to.

Get a seamless experience

With iPhone you can sync and share your pictures, music, apps and more across all your Apple devices. They’re all designed to work seamlessly together, to make your life as easy as possible. Listen to your favourite tracks, check out your photos and open your apps wherever you are - with iCloud, whatever you need is at your fingertips.

Have fun with your phone

Watch videos on the web. Track your fitness goals. Chat to your friends. With over 2 million apps to download in the App Store, whatever you do for fun there’s bound to be an app to help you. And if you need to add a social event to your calendar when you’re on the move, just ask Siri.
